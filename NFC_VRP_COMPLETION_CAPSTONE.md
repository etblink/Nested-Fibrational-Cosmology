*Produced L+47 as Project Lead, marking the terminal in-toolkit state of
the Variable Recovery Program (VRP), built across sessions L+23–L+46 in
NFC Book III. This is a consolidated governance/summary document, not a
new canonical claim; every result it summarizes carries its stated
status and conditional hypotheses in Book III. The Canon Ledger remains
the authoritative session record.*

---

## 1. What the program set out to do

The VRP licenses physics-variable *vocabulary* — energy, time, mass,
charge, and so on — as recovered structure rather than imported
primitives. Each variable is admitted only through one of the four
disjuncts of the master licensing predicate (Book VII): a
quotient-visible invariant, a transport-stable ledger, a certified
branch-interface coefficient, or a continuum encoding of certified
discrete structure — with scope, descent path, status, and an
anti-smuggling audit declared in each case. The program does **not**
derive physics, solve any branch's hard problem, or assert physical
law; it reconstructs how the standard physics variables can be
*licensed* from observational/spectral structure already canonical in
the spine and branches.

## 3. The variables are internally coupled — five bindings

The recovered variables are not independent posits; the program proved
five inter-variable bindings:
1. **Generator ↔ order** (thm:vrp-ham-time-realization, L+27): the
   H_B-generated family realizes branch time exactly on sectors where
   ⪯_H = ⪯_t, with ledger-visible failure diagnosis.
2. **Entropy ↔ order** (thm:vrp-entropy-clock, L+28): the canonical
   cross-chain clock is the entropy/defect ledger; arrow and clock are
   one object.
3. **Three-variable sector** (thm:vrp-mass-sector, L+30): the nontrivial
   mass sector = persistent span ⊖ kernel, built from entropy ledger,
   continuation order, and generator together.
4. **Charge ↔ order** (cor:vrp-charge-conservation, L+45): charge is
   conserved along the licensed continuation order.
5. **Frequency ↔ generator/mass** (cor:vrp-frequency-energy-binding,
   L+46): mass is the bottom spectral gap, frequency the full gap
   lattice — two readings of one spectrum.

## 4. The deepest structural results

- **The realization criterion** (L+27): "evolution generated by H_B"
  has licensed meaning exactly on monotone-realization sectors;
  elsewhere the failure is a named, ledger-visible object.
- **Clock = arrow** (L+28): the Book I irreversible temporal preorder is
  the spine instance of the entropy clock read as order.
- **Entropy transfer is absolute** (L+29): lax-functorial with no
  exchange coefficient — the absence of a scale freedom is itself
  canonical information distinguishing entropy from energy.
- **Forced kernel factorization** (L+30): the naive mass sector fails a
  coercivity audit (persistent zero-cost labels); factoring out ker H_B
  is forced, and the discarded part is recovered as superselection-type
  structure.
- **Two distinct times** (L+32, L+33): thermodynamic time (affine gauge:
  unit+origin) and reversible/unitary time (cyclic gauge:
  origin+orientation) are provably distinct licensing problems; the
  reversible-clock dichotomy shows unitary motion is either pure gauge
  or cyclic-phase, never global linear time.
- **Energy form = weight declaration** (L+34): a branch carries a
  ledger-level energy form iff it declares weighted transport-coercion
  structure; the canonical persistence cost is the maximal subordinate
  functional, choice-free.
- **The synchronization obstruction** (L+36): phase synchronization and
  the temperature zeroth law are one cohomology class in H^1(interface
  nerve; G), supported on β_1 — the same cycle rank that
  thm:beta1-controls-nonabelian assigns to gauge structure.
  Synchronization is free exactly where there is no gauge structure to
  obstruct it.
- **Charge discreteness from finite type structure** (L+45): charges are
  discrete because types are — quantization as a consequence of the
  finite type-transition operator, not an external postulate.
- **Phase–frequency Pontryagin duality** (L+46): the phase torus and the
  frequency module are character-lattice dual — abelian duality, with
  ℏ, uncertainty bounds, and E=ℏω explicitly excluded by scope clause.

## 5. What remains open or conditional (honest boundary)

**In-program open obligation (1):** O_Charge.Quant-cont
(ob:vrp-charge-quant) — continuum survival of finite-regime spectral
discreteness, covering both the charge spectrum and the frequency
module (one continuum-bridge question over two objects). In-toolkit;
the continuum-bridge instance of the standing Book VI question.

**All licensing theorems are [C]:** each carries named conditional
hypotheses (lawful branch, declared realization, finite spectral
support, etc.). This is by design and is not a deficiency — recovering a
variable from canonical inputs cannot be unconditional unless those
inputs are, and Book VII's promotion law forbids self-promotion to
unconditional closure.

**External-machinery frontier (out of toolkit, per L+42/43 triage):**
the four reduced-frontier items — RH arithmetic orbit grammar, RH
packet-local synthesis, SM matter content (gated on post-program YM
quantization), GR global curvature-subcriticality — each require
machinery categorically outside the NFC observational/collar-algebra
apparatus. The VRP did not touch these; they are the standing
external-machinery register.

**Ladder rungs not built (assessed at/beyond the toolkit boundary,
L+46):** Metric/Distance and Curvature require the spacetime geometry
Book VI only derives conditionally; Momentum and Spin require the metric
or the gated E_8/triality representation layer. Building any of these
from conditionally-derived machinery would violate the feasibility
discipline that governed every rung actually built. The cleanly
in-toolkit rungs are exhausted at Frequency/Phase.

**NFR (Nested Fibrational Realization):** assessed L+44 as not promotable
(fails Book V branch legitimacy and Book VII promotion law; self-
disclaims both); its legitimate role is a cross-branch defect-
localization tracking instrument (obligation roster §6), and it sharpens
but does not discharge the YM/GR/SM residual defects.

## 6. Terminal state

The in-toolkit recovery program is complete: eight licensed variables,
five inter-variable bindings, the synchronization obstruction unifying
the residual frontier with gauge structure, and a documented, honest
toolkit boundary. Governance is reconciled (obligation roster, four
tracking conventions catalogued, namespace defect fixed), and the
corpus's frontier accounting is mechanically auditable.

The program's contribution is the *reductions and licensings*, executed
under anti-smuggling discipline, with every boundary named rather than
crossed by fiat. What lies beyond — the four external-machinery frontier
items and the geometry/representation rungs — is left honestly external,
which is the integrity of the result, not its limitation.
