By the no-hidden-supplementation requirement of Book V branch-legitimacy (thm:VII.6.4), every structural pathway in a lawful branch must be declared in the branch's import ledger. Applied to hereditary pathways: any undeclared hereditary channel would constitute a prohibited import (Book VII, def:prohibited-import). Since \Phi{}_Bio is the complete declared replication record, no channel outside \Phi{}_Bio has canonical force in the branch.
