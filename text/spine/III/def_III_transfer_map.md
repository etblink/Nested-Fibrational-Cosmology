A transfer map is a structure-preserving morphism phi: Obs(U_n) -> Obs(U_{n+1}) between persistent observables across different regimes. A transfer map must be: (1) certified: established by a theorem or declared by a branch-specific hypothesis; (2) compatibility-preserving: carrying the declared compatibility structure; (3) transport-invariant: preserving the canonical transport invariant I_nl (def:IV.transport-invariant). Transfer maps are the morphisms of the OT (Observational Transfer) category, which is the precursor to the POT category of Book IV.
