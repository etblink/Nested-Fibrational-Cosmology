The Boundary-Uniform Overlap condition BUO(delta) holds if there exists delta > 0 such that for every pair of adjacent collar types [beta], [beta'] in Sigma_partial, the type-walk transition T_w([beta],[beta']) >= delta. Equivalently: the type walk has uniform positive overlap on adjacent collar types. The constant delta is computable from the NF_2 type table. BUO(delta) is the hypothesis feeding lem:III.cheeger-from-BUO (spectral gap) and thm:III.ACA-chain (ACA coercivity chain). It is also the hypothesis cited by thm:VI.NS-4B-sobolev (the NS-4B Sobolev surrogate in Book VI).
