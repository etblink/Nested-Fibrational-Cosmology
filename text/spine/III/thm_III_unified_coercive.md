Unified Coercive-Transport Inequality [U]: for every admissible enlargement U_n -> U_{n+1}, C_{n+1}(U_{n+1}) <= Theta_n(U_n) * C_n(U_n) + E_n(U_n) + B_n(U_n), where Theta_n in [0,1] is the transport factor, E_n is the transported internal defect, and B_n is the interface boundary defect. The three sources are: (1) transported bulk (discounted by Theta_n); (2) interior defect from Book I ledger additivity; (3) boundary defect from Book II LS-2 boundary law. This theorem is the load-bearing core of Book III. It is fully branch-agnostic: it depends only on the defect ledger of Book I and the LS-2 boundary law of Book II. Branch books specialize it by fixing specific forms of Theta_n, E_n, B_n; those specializations belong in the branch books, not here.
