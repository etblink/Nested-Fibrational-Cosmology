Named Failure Frontier Theorem: Rephrasing an open obligation does not constitute progress on it. The canon's named failure frontiers (NS Stage-3, RH S1-ARC, YM B3-A2, GR global subcriticality, etc.) can be discharged only by explicit new theorems that meet the burden as stated. A new description of the same gap, a weaker version of the result, or a claim that the gap is smaller than previously thought do not constitute discharge. This theorem is the formal ground for the inadmissibility of the following discharge modes for frontiers: renaming only, prose softening, table omission.
