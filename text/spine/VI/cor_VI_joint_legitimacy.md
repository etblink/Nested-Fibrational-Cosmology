Joint Legitimacy of Full Calculus [C]: for a certified observable family in a lawful continuum interface regime, the full calculus of differential and integral operators is jointly legitimate as asymptotic encoding of the certified discrete dynamics. This corollary is what branch books actually invoke when they write PDEs, variational principles, or Sobolev norms: the full calculus is jointly licensed by Thm. VI.9.1 for observables in the declared interface regime.
