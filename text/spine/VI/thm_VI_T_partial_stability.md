Perturbation Stability of T_partial [U]: the type-transition operator T_partial: V -> V is stable under small perturbations of the admissible collar substrate. If T_partial' is the operator of a substrate differing by perturbation of size epsilon, then ||T_partial' - T_partial||_op <= delta_stab * epsilon, where delta_stab = K_0 = 7 is table-computable. The eigenvalues of T_partial are Lipschitz in the perturbation with constant 7. This theorem is [U] because K_0=7 is proved unconditionally (thm:I.K0-prime) and Phase-A follows (cor:II.ls2-universal-K07). This is the primary structural stability result of Book VI and feeds into the screened-sector identification of the YM and NS branches.
