Continuum Bridge Schema Theorem [U]: a branch may promote a certified discrete result to continuum force only when: (1) a scoped certificate establishes the result on the source discrete regime, AND (2) a separately proved continuum bridge carries that certified content to the declared continuum target. Neither ingredient alone suffices: a scoped certificate alone establishes only the discrete claim; a continuum bridge alone has no certified source content to carry. Both are required. This theorem is the canonical gate for all continuum-language use in the NFC program. It is unconditional: it is a structural fact about what is required for continuum promotion, not a claim that any particular promotion is valid.
