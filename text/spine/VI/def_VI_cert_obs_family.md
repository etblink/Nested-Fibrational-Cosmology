A certified observable family is an observable family whose stabilized quotient class is invariant under the declared admissible test family and which has been explicitly certified as the input to a continuum bridge. Certification requires: (1) the family is source-descended from U; (2) the stabilized quotient is well-defined (Book I); (3) the family satisfies the declared admissibility conditions (Book II). A certified observable family is the canonical input object for any continuum bridge construction.
