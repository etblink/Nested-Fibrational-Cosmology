Ledger-Entropy Duality Theorem: for a stabilized survivor chain chi, the total defect equals the entropy decrease: D(chi) = H_0 - H_inf. The defect ledger and the structural entropy are dual accounting systems: every unit of defect corresponds to one unit of entropy decrease. This duality gives the defect a clean information-theoretic interpretation: it measures how much observational information is lost as the refinement chain progresses to stabilization.
