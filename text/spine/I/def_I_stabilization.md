A refinement chain (T_n)_{n>=0} stabilizes at stage N if Sigma_n = Sigma_N for all n >= N. The stable limit is Sigma_inf := Sigma_N, the stabilized observational quotient. Theorem thm:I.stabilization proves that every refinement chain on a finite configuration space stabilizes; the stabilized quotient Sigma_inf is therefore well-defined (cor:I-stabilized-welldef). This is one of the two primary unconditional exports of Book I.
