Two configurations omega and omega' in Omega are observationally equivalent at stage n (written omega ~_n omega') if no test in the licensed test family T_n separates them: for all T in T_n, T(omega) = T(omega'). This is the canonical NFC equivalence relation at stage n. It is finer than structural equivalence (def:struct-equiv) because tests can detect behavioral distinctions that signature alone does not capture.
