Shared Coercive Transport Stage 1 (SCT.1): the first stage of the coercive transport doctrine. Under Phase-A, the coercive structure of the boundary-capacity bound can be transported from stage n to stage n+1 along a stabilized collar chain. SCT.1 establishes the base case for the inductive transport argument. It is the Book II source of the SCT.1-SCT.6b chain imported by both the NS branch (for the safe-tail discharge of ob:NS.SB2) and the SCC branch (for the structural counterfactual capacity argument).
