The Local Admissible Realizability (LAR) condition at radius rho: every collar type in the stabilized collar alphabet is locally realizable — there exists a configuration omega such that [del_rho(U) * omega]_inf equals that collar type. LAR at radius 2 implies that the boundary-law map Phi_U sending stabilized local outcomes to stabilized collar types is injective on realized outcomes. This injectivity is required for the LS-2 proof (Step 3 in thm:II.ls2-from-conditions).
