LS-2 (Locality Hypothesis): there exists a finite radius rho* such that for every admissible local question on a region U, once the refinement program has stabilized, the observable local outcome on U is determined by the stabilized collar type on del_{rho*}(U). This is the principal conditional hinge of the NFC corpus. Every downstream book that carries conditional force inherits it primarily from LS-2. For canonical K_0=7 Phase-A substrates, LS-2 holds universally (cor:II.ls2-universal-K07, [U]). In the general case, LS-2 remains a named conditional hypothesis. The radius rho*=2 is proved sufficient under DW+TC+LAR (thm:II.ls2-from-conditions).
