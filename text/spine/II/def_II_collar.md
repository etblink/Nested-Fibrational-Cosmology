The collar of a region U at radius rho is the rho-neighborhood boundary del_rho(U): the set of points within distance rho of the boundary of U but outside U. The stabilized collar type of U is the equivalence class [del_rho(U)]_inf under the stabilized observational equivalence from Book I. The collar structure is the primary local boundary datum in Book II: all local observable claims are controlled by the stabilized collar type.
