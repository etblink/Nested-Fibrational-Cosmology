Three structural phases are defined for admissible systems: Phase-A = bounded propagation (collar influence decays with radius) + LS-2 + finite recurrence (every collar type recurs with bounded gap); Phase-B = unbounded propagation regime; Phase-C = collapse regime. Phase-A is the canonical conditional hypothesis throughout the NFC program. The governing theorem of Book II (thm:II.governing) holds conditionally on Phase-A. The YM, NS, and GR branches all carry Phase-A as an inherited hypothesis.
