Binary Rigidity: the binary toggle structure (E+/E-) is rigid: the only admissible generator of the parity symmetry is binary. Proof: the toggle-complete structure forces the generator orbit to partition the collar positions into pairs; the parity symmetry is generated by binary toggles and no coarser partition is admissible. This theorem provides the structural basis for the two-eigenvalue (E+/E-) decomposition that appears throughout the branch books.
