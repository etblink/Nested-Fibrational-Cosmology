Shared Coercive Transport Stage 6b (SCT.6b): the final stage of the coercive transport doctrine. Under Phase-A, when the three SCT.6b firing conditions (coercive structure, transport compatibility, subcriticality check) are satisfied for a tail window, the positive reserve on the safe tail (SB2) is discharged. SCT.6b is the Book II source of the discharge of ob:NS.SB2 in the NS branch (thm:NS.SB2-discharged) and contributes to the SCC conditional cert-close (thm:SCC.TSI).
