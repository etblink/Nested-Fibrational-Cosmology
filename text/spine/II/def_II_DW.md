The Dominated-Witness (DW) condition: the local admissible behavior inside a region U is controlled by collar-type propagation. Precisely: any two configurations with the same stabilized collar type on del_rho(U) produce the same sequence of stabilization steps inside U, so their stabilized local outcomes are equal. DW is one of three conditions (with TC and LAR) that together imply LS-2. DW is proved unconditionally from Axiom A2 (thm:II.DW-from-A2).
