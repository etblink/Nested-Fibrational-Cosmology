Local Nuclear Theorem: for any finite relational system, the admissible observational behavior has exactly two regimes — local and nuclear. Local regime: the admissible class acts freely on Sigma_inf (every collar type is reachable from every other). Nuclear regime: any admissible class has a fixed collar type (a collar type preserved by all admissible generators). The two regimes are mutually exclusive and together cover all cases. This unconditional partition is the Book II structural basis for the phase structure definition.
