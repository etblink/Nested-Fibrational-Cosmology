DW from Structural Invariance: the Dominated-Witness condition follows unconditionally from Axiom A2 (structural invariance) of the admissible class. Proof: A2 states that structurally equivalent configurations are treated identically by all admissible processes. If two configurations have the same stabilized collar type, they are structurally equivalent at the collar. By A2, all admissible processes produce the same outcomes inside U. Therefore collar-type propagation controls local behavior, which is exactly DW.
