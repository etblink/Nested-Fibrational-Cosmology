An endpoint datum E = F(U) is the certified image of the universal source object U under the declared realization functor F. It is well-defined by prop:V.branch-projection. It is the canonical claim-object of a branch book: the datum that the branch asserts it has derived from U. The endpoint datum must be branch-visible (sr:V.endpoint-visibility) and must satisfy the no-hidden-supplementation rule (sr:V.no-hidden-supplementation). All five UBLT conditions constrain properties of the endpoint datum.
