Mandatory Branch Declarations Corollary: every derived branch book must declare five items before any endpoint claim: (1) its licensed target category C; (2) its realization functor F: POT -> C and the bridge theorem licensing it; (3) its endpoint datum E = F(U); (4) the native equivalence of C determining when two endpoint data coincide; (5) its legitimacy witness. A branch book failing to supply these five items is not canonically admissible under Book V. This corollary converts the UBLT from a biconditional into a checklist: the mandatory declarations are exactly the information needed to verify all five UBLT conditions.
