The POT category is the category whose objects are POT objects and whose morphisms are POT-morphisms (structure-preserving maps between POT objects that commute with the transfer structure and preserve defect bookkeeping). The POT category is the canonical source category of the NFC program: it is the domain of all realization functors F: POT -> C used by branch books. The existence and structure of U as a POT object is proved in thm:IV.universal-completion.
