Universal Completion Theorem (Thm. IV.5.1): U = T/~_pres is a universal completion of D_pers, satisfying five properties: (a) Observable factoring — for every POT object X and compatible cocone, there exists a unique morphism U -> X (terminal direction = colimit); (b) Coherent presentation — all enlargement-compatible presentations map coherently into U; (c) Minimality — U is minimal among POT objects receiving all fiber data coherently; (d) Realization initiality — every faithful realization F: POT -> C is determined by F(U); (e) Uniqueness — U is unique up to unique POT-isomorphism. This theorem is the primary existence result for the universal source. Property (d) is the Book IV source of UBLT condition 2 (certified source descent: E = F(U)).
