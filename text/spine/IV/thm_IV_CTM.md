Coherent Transition-Map Theorem (CTM): for each admissible enlargement iota: R -> R', there exists a unique canonical POT-morphism tau_iota: F_R -> F_{R'} satisfying identity, composition, and ORA compatibility. Consequently the assignment R |-> F_R, iota |-> tau_iota is a functor D_pers: Reg -> POT (the persistent diagram). This functor is the diagram whose colimit is U. The CTM converts the collection of fiber objects into a genuine directed diagram of POT objects — the prerequisite for the universal completion construction.
