Completion Ladder Theorem: the five ORA rungs (IV.2.1 through IV.5.3 in the original numbering) are jointly sufficient to construct the universal completion U. This theorem packages the five rung theorems (ORA, CTM, normalization, AMCT, and the colimit construction) into a single assertion that their joint force is sufficient. It is the immediate predecessor of thm:IV.universal-completion and provides the construction scaffolding.
